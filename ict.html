About TVL–ICT

The TVL–ICT Strand prepares students for careers in technology through hands-on training and real-world learning experiences. It is ideal for students who enjoy coding, web design, and computer servicing.

Main Goals

  • Equip students with strong technical computer skills
  • Provide real-world ICT training
  • Prepare learners for employment or IT college programs

What Students Learn

Computer System Servicing

  • Assembling and maintaining computers
  • Installing operating systems
  • Troubleshooting hardware and software

Programming & Coding

  • Basic coding skills
  • Software development fundamentals

Web Development

  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Responsive web design

Multimedia & Digital Design

  • Graphic design
  • Photo & video editing

Career Opportunities

  • Computer Technician
  • IT Support Staff
  • Web Developer
  • Graphic Designer
  • Programmer
  • Multimedia Editor
